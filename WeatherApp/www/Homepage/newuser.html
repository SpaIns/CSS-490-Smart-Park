Smart Park

How to use Smart Park


Step 1: Select a parking garage to view the available parking spots per floor.

Step 2: Select a floor to view a live map of the current occupancy.

Step 3: From there you can navigate through the levels by going up or down a floor, or return to the main page.

Don't forget to set your preferences through the gear on the navigation bar!

DISCLAIMER: Due to technological constraints we currently do not offer data for the root-level lots, including North Garage level 4 and South Garage level 5. Venture at your own risk!

Thank you for using our app!
